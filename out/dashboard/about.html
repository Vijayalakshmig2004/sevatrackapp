Our Mission

SevaTrack was created by Vijayalakshmi to make public grievance redressal easier for citizens. The portal helps people register civic problems, attach proof, save the issue location, and follow each stage until a service partner completes the work and the complaint is closed.

Why SevaTrack?

Easy Submission

Submit grievances in minutes with our intuitive form and category selection.

Real-time Tracking

Track your complaint status in real-time with detailed timeline updates.

Secure & Private

Your data is encrypted and protected with industry-standard security.

Fast Resolution

Automated routing ensures your complaint reaches the right department quickly.
